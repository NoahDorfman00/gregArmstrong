Mr. Smooth

Greg Armstrong is the quintessential crooner of your local scene, bringing the timeless charm of the Rat Pack to life. With a voice that echoes the smooth, velvety tones of Dean Martin, the suave sophistication of Frank Sinatra, the vibrant energy of Bobby Darin, and the electrifying charisma of Elvis Presley, Greg captivates audiences at restaurants and private events. Dressed in classic style, Greg transports his listeners back to the golden era of music with every performance. Whether he's crooning "That's Amore" or swinging through "Mack the Knife," his renditions are both authentic and enchanting. His performances are not just musical experiences but a journey through the heart of American pop culture, where each song tells a story and every note is a nod to the legends he emulates. Perfect for intimate gatherings or setting the mood in a bustling restaurant, Greg Armstrong is the go-to entertainer for those looking to add a touch of nostalgia and elegance to their event.
